# 📱 Social Media Etiquette Guide
> **Role:** Student Digital Ambassador  
> **Goal:** Helping peers maintain a professional and safe digital footprint.

---

### ✨ The "Do's" (Best Practices)
* 🔒 **Audit Your Privacy Settings:** Regularly review who can see your posts and personal information on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
* 🧠 **Think Before You Post:** Remember that your digital footprint can be permanent; ensure your content reflects a professional image.
* 🤝 **Maintain a Respectful Tone:** Treat every online interaction with the same level of respect as a face-to-face conversation.
* 🔍 **Verify Before Sharing:** Always fact-check news or links before reposting to prevent the spread of misinformation.
* 🌐 **Network Purposefully:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow industry leaders and engage with academic communities.

---

### ⚠️ The "Don'ts" (Common Pitfalls)
* 🚫 **Don't Overshare Sensitive Data:** Never post your home address, phone number, or current real-time location publicly.
* 📉 **Don't Engage in Cyber-Incivility:** Avoid "flaming" or getting into heated public arguments that could damage your reputation.
* 🖱️ **Don't Click Unverified Links:** Be wary of "too good to be true" offers or suspicious DMs that could be phishing attempts.
* 👔 **Don't Neglect Professionalism:** Avoid posting content that depicts unprofessional behavior that could alert college authorities.
* 📝 **Don't Plagiarize Content:** Always give credit when sharing someone else's creative work, research, or ideas.

---

### 🛡️ Pro-Tip for Ambassadors
> "Your online presence is your virtual resume. Build it with the same care you would use for a physical one!"
